Gamification — Not Everyone Is Wired for It

  • Gamification sounds great in theory, but people respond very differently
  • Weekly sales leaderboard? Some reps thrive — others find it stressful
  • Team-based training simulations? Fun for some — a chore for others
  • If someone doesn't find it engaging, it's not a game — it's just extra work
Personality matters — one size does not fit all.

What Real Gamification Looks Like

  • Pokémon Go: players think they're just catching Pokémon
  • But Niantic collects their location data, photos, and videos — all voluntarily — to train AI models
  • Players never realize they are doing work
True gamification means people never notice they are being trained.
