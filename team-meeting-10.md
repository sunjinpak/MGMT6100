### Today's Debate Topic

**"Are labor unions still necessary in today's workplace?"**

- **Employee Advocates:** Argue that unions protect workers' rights, ensure fair wages, provide collective bargaining power, and address workplace safety concerns.

- **Employer Advocates:** Argue that modern labor laws already protect workers, unions can create adversarial relationships, increase costs, and reduce organizational flexibility.
